ABOUT US

ClayConcept is a design agency focused on providing streamlined design services for Start-Ups Globally. Our focus is on providing young companies with a design service that scales with them, no year-long contracts, no hidden fees, just a fixed monthly fee for our design services with the option to pause, cancel or scale up and down with the changing needs of a companies growth.

We make it easier for start-ups to budget R&D, innovation, and branding.

ClayConcept is growing but in order to ensure quality design, we currently focus on helping small to mid-size start-ups, companies and non-profits.

We are currently shifting from private services to a global online design service. Our full website is expected to launch & hire more team members by January of 2025.

About the Founder

Jon Prado is a designer embracing the values of Social & Ecological Responsibility.

Armed with degrees in both Fine Art and Industrial Design, Jon utilizes his expertise in Design Thinking, User Experience, Aesthetics & Rapid Prototyping to make innovation accessible for all.

Beyond his work in the US, Jon is collaborating with innovators across the globe, currently residing in Southeast Asia. Jon actively collaborates with Medical, Non-Profits and Education organizations, lending his expertise to enhance their Product Development, User Experiences, Branding & Digital Communication Interfaces.
